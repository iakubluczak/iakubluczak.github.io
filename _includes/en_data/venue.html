Ceremony Venue

We’re getting married in a chapel located in a residential building. Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the ceremony so you can settle in, soak up the atmosphere, and enjoy the moment. The chapel has seating, a restroom, and drinks available if needed. And if you’re not religious — no worries. Everyone is warmly welcome.

Tip: Parking nearby is limited, so please keep that in mind. You can leave your car at the shopping mall parking lot, about a 5-minute walk away. If you’re not driving, there are plenty of other ways to get there. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
